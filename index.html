ABACUS Club: CODM Tournament Rules and Guidelines

GUIDELINES

· All Grade 11 and Grade 12 non-ICT and ICT students are eligible to join this contest.

· Teams that do haven’t same section/strand are allowed to build a team

· Same section/strand teams is allowed also

· A team should have 6 members (5 Players and 1 Coach)

· Deadline of Submission is on March 2, 2024, 10:00 am – 5:00 pm

· No player switching in the game. (Ex: Player 1 playing on Player 2’s phone)

· Teams will be given three (3) pause opportunities with a maximum of 2 minutes. (Can be exceeded depending on the situation)

· Internet access will be provided by the players.

· Brackets will based on the number of registered participants.

· The maximum of registered teams will be 16 teams only.

· Fail to attend the game will forfeit their registration.

· An officer will be assigned to each team to monitor their activities.

· Competing teams will play 3 modes below and teams can choose and ban maps according to the following modes to compete with:

  • Hardpoint
  • Search and Destroy
  • Control

· These are the map pools that teams can choose from the designated modes per game

  • Apocalypse, Hacienda, Ice Breaker, Rush, Express, Takeoff, Crossfire, Rust, Crash, Satellite, Suldal Harbor, Shipment and Summit

· Eliminations and Playoff Bracket are on Best of Three (3) while Finals are on Best of Five (5)

· Winners will be announced at the Culminating.
